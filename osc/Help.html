Tips for using this template:
2
1. Raw data goes to the "Data" sheet. "Timestamp" is the time data is posted, "Timepoint" is the time data is sent from the device.
3
2. Number format in the "Timepoint" column must be a decimal number for the chart to plot properly.
4
3. "Report" sheet formats data newest to oldest and allows unit conversion from Farhenheit to Celsius and SG to Plato.
5
4. "Report" sheet also allows viewing multiple time frames for higher level detail.
6
5. Some cells have warnings on making changes, this is to prevent accidental edits that would make the sheet not work.
7
6. Comments on sheet help explain why some of the calculations are there. Comments may be deleted if desired.
